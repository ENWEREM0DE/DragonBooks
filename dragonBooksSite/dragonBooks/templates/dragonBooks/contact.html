  • eTextbook

    What is an eTextbook?

    An eTextbook (also known as a digital textbook or eText) is a digital, downloadable version of a printed textbook. eTextbooks can be read online or offline through any supported device (PC, Mac, Smartphone, or Tablet running iOS or Android, Kindle Fire, and Chromebook). eTextbook readers have interactive features including highlights, notes, flashcards and read aloud that allow for an engaging reading experience

    What devices can I read an eTextbook on?

    Read your textbooks on any computer, tablet or mobile phone. VitalSource Bookshelf is compatible with both Macs and PCs as well as iOS, Android, Kindle Fire, and Chromebook devices.

    Can I access my eTextbook on more than one device?

    Yes. You will be able to download and access your eTextbook onto 2 computers AND 2 mobile devices using the Bookshelf app.

    Do I have to be connected to the internet to read my eTextbook?

    No! You can access 100% of your eTextbooks without an internet connection

    Returns & Refunds

    We want you to enjoy your purchase from the DragonsBooks Store. However, if you need to return an item, we're here to help.

    You're welcome to return your textbook for a full refund within 14 days from the date of purchase.

    Standard Return Policy

    In order to receive your DragonsBooks refund

    • Book must be in the same condition in which we shipped it to you. We're not responsible for any damage incurred during transit. When preparing your package for shipment, you may choose to purchase insurance for your package from any UPS store or your post office.
    • You must connect to your seller to be returned.

    There are a few important things to keep in mind when returning a book, you purchased from the DragonsBooks Store:

    • You have 14 calendar days to return an item from the date you purchased it.
    • You have not viewed or printed, in total, more than twenty percent (20%) of the DragonsBooks eTextbook.
    • You have not used any included access codes for any online resource that came with your eTextbook purchase.
    • You are not returning an eBook from one of the publishers in our Return Policy Exceptions.
    • You can only return items purchased directly from the DragonsBooks Store or contacting to the seller.

    Returns for DragonsBooks eBooks purchases through other retailers are subject to their respective returns and refunds policy.

    Abuse of Return Policy: DragonsBooks reserves the right to refuse to sell or to provide refunds to any person for any reason not prohibited by law, including, but not limited to, abuse of its return policy.

    Refunds

    Once we receive your refund request, a refund is initiated immediately. We refund your purchase back to the original payment method.

    We will send credit and debit card refunds to the card-issuing bank within five to seven business days of receipt of the return. Please contact the card-issuing bank with questions about when you can expect the credit to post on your account.
