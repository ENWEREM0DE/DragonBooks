About Dragon Book

Dragon Books is a one-stop ecommerce search engine that searches for available books for sale - new, used, rare, out of print and textbooks. We save your time and money by searching every major catalog online and letting you know which booksellers are offering the best prices and selection. When you find a book you like, you can buy it directly from the original seller, we never charge a markup.

Dragon Books was launched in 2021 by your fellow students who were looking for an alternative options to buy books from and avoid the high prices from big institutions.
